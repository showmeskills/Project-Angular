Job Description:

1.Acts as a strategic HR partner to ensure manpower growth, development, and stability of the assigned business unit.

2.Ensures that all hiring and onboarding operations are on track for the assigned business unit.

3.Coordinates with various People Team points of contact to resolve employee concerns.

4.Ensures People Team's compliance with internal and external regulatory requirements and HR audits, as needed.

5.Manages employee case resolution in coordination with the Employee Relations team.

6.Assists with implementation of new and ongoing business initiatives, as needed.

7.Maintains open communication channels with stakeholders and be willing to cater to urgent employee requests potentially outside regular working hours.





Requirements:

1.Demonstrated success with setting up and providing strategic HR support.

2.Bachelor's degree in any field post-graduate degree in Industrial Relations, Labor Relations, or other course in Human Resources is a plus.

3.Prior experience as an HR Generalist or equivalent cross-functional exposure in different HR facets.

4.Strong background in Recruitment is a plus .

5.Great team player and works well with others.

6.Strong mindset for continuous improvement and exceeding client expectations.

7.Operates with a high degree of integrity and stellar work ethics.

8.Capability to work in a multicultural environment is a must.
