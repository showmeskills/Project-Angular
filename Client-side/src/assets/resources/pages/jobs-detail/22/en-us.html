Job Responsibilities:

1. Responsible for the construction of the customer service team's operation system and daily management, and responsible for the service level, user satisfaction, and team performance achievement rate.

2. According to the requirements of product upgrade and adjustment and process change, make corresponding language adjustments and improvements in a timely manner, formulate implementation plans, help the staff of the customer service department to quickly familiarize themselves with the changes, and put forward effective opinions on the sorting out of business processes.

3. Collect, count, and analyze customer service issues and data, feed back to the service operation department, and constantly propose improvements to the product level from a business perspective, focusing on problem prevention and analysis and solutions to problems that have occurred.

4. Regularly track and evaluate data, and submit relevant business analysis reports, put forward improvement measures in a timely manner, and give practical and reliable improvement plans.

5. Responsible for employee recruitment, interviews, on-the-job business coaching and assessment, regularly formulate study plans, set assessment goals, and help employees continue to improve.

6. Responsible for coordinating business relations with other business departments and cooperating with the development of cross-departmental work.

4. Quick thinking, good communication and coordination skills, team management skills, leadership skills, stress resistance, and upward management skills.

5. Personality needs to have the courage to accept challenges, have a strong internal drive and initiative, and pursue excellence in doing things.

6. Well-known first-line Internet-related work experience, fluent in English, reading, writing and speaking is preferred.
