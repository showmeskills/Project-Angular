Professional skills:

1. Game product and event project management.

2. Possess project management ability, responsible for product pre-operation, schedule planning, maintenance execution and quality management.

3. Plan promotions, from design to execution to effectiveness evaluation.

5. Handle product communication and coordination with relevant departments and multinational manufacturers.

6. More than 3 years experience in game product manager.

7. Have experience in product strategy formulation, be proactive, be able to master team resource scheduling, and the ability to communicate and collaborate across departments.

Personality traits:

Excellent data analysis and logical thinking skills; executive and self-driven, ability to withstand pressure; excellent communication and project management skills.
