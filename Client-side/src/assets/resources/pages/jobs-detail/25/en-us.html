1. 2. Participate in major company decisions, predict future organizational needs, provide positive and effective suggestions and opinions from the perspective of human resources, provide organizational diagnosis and intellectual support for strategy implementation, control human resource costs, and improve human efficiency. And participate in the implementation of the strategy.

Comprehensively plan the human resources development and strategic management of the whole group ( Taiwan, the Philippines, Dubai, Malta), improve the collaborative management mechanism between the group and its subsidiaries, clarify the human resources management goals and overall plans, and formulate Human resource planning and overseeing the implementation of various plans.
